Today, I wear many hats: a Course Facilitator who has trained over 100 students in coding, web design, and digital creativity; a Content Creator who crafts compelling stories for digital platforms; and a Digital Journalist writing headline news for Look at Us.

I am passionate about the intersection of technology and education — using digital tools to unlock potential in young people across Nigeria and beyond. I am fluent in English and conversational in Yoruba and Igbo.
